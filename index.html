About VapurCut

VapurCut is a lightweight mobile app that removes photo backgrounds using on-device AI. All image processing happens directly on your device — no uploads, no servers, and no data sharing.

Key Features

  • AI-powered background removal
  • Fully on-device processing
  • No account or login required
  • Fast and privacy-focused
  • Works offline after install
