VapurCut Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

VapurCut respects your privacy. This privacy policy explains how information is handled when you use the VapurCut mobile application.

1. Image Processing

All photos selected or captured in VapurCut are processed entirely on your device. Images are not uploaded, not transmitted, and not stored on any external server.

2. Data Collection

VapurCut does not collect, store, or share any personal data. The app does not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, contacts, device identifiers, or analytics data.

3. Permissions

VapurCut requests only the minimum permissions required for its core functionality:

Camera permission is used solely to capture photos for background removal.

Media / Storage permission is used only to select photos from your device and to save processed images back to your device.

4. Third-Party Services
